Design Implementation

Fonts

The font choices needed to relfect the balance between corporate sites and easy of readability while at the same time reflect the swiss international(mod-ish) design style. Thus, the following fonts were chosen:

  • Roboto

  • Roboto Slab

  • Roboto Mono

  • Roboto Condensed

Trade-Offs

A choice was made to use those design items on desktop, that while were not available on mobile, could be implemented in a different way on mobile. Thus, getting as close as possible to delivering fully equal viewing experiences on both desktop and mobile.

Sometimes, that meant avoiding the choice of a verrtical slider and implementing a better designed horizontal slider design on desktop in order to bring the same viewing experience to mobile. Soemtimes, it meant avoiding a choice of a vertically offset card overlay as a section title and section description on desktop in order to give the same viewing expereience to both desktop and mobile on viewing that section title and section description.

Design Vision

Modernism

The Modernism movement is not just something that makes you squint hard at a Musuem painting trying to determine what meaning the painter meant. It's a human ambition to derive a new meaning that does not rely upon un-factual inventions but human mind inventions to understand the factual universe.

In a blink of an eye, the web-and-internet implementation became the new modernism movement.The inter-connective-ness of the different tribes and that freedom to connect, relate, and improve knowledge becoem the extension of modernims itself. One of the ways I can visualize that for the viewer is not limiting web links to just ones for the website itself.

The Web Movement, Successor to Modernism

Rather than the un-factual one-family tribe against all others, what does a group of inter-connected tribes look like as a human movement? Yes, one can say the web itself is that movement but how does one convey that visually in the web-interface set of implementations?

Transitions help me visuallize for the website viewer the impression that the website and the web-internet-interface is breathing-living enitity that, unlike the ships in aliens, travels with us on our journey to improve the human race and the universe. My design vision goal is to show that we have this living and breathing organisms of web that ravels with us to improve human-kind and the universe.
